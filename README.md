# Nexleaders: City Management Simulator

Nexleaders is a strategy and simulation game where players take on the role of a city manager. The goal is to balance the growth and development of the city with its environmental impact and citizen satisfaction.

## 🎮 Gameplay
In Nexleaders, every decision counts. Players must monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and manage the city's resources effectively. You will face various choices that directly impact the state of your city.

## 📊 Key Features
- City Dashboard: Real-time monitoring of essential statistics:
  - AQI (Air Quality Index): Monitor air pollution levels.
  - Sea Pollution: Manage waste and protect marine life.
  - Citizen Satisfaction: Keep the residents happy.
  - Occupancy: Balance housing and resource distribution.
- Decision-Making System: Engage with dynamic scenarios that test your management skills.
- Strategy Simulation: Think critically about the consequences of your environmental and urban planning policies.

## 🛠️ Built With
- Engine: Unity
- Language: C#
- Platform: PC

## 🚀 How to Run
1. Clone this repository.
2. Open the project in Unity Hub (ensure you have the correct Unity version).
3. Open the main scene and press Play.
